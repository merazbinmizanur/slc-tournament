The Bounty Protocol

Welcome to the SLC BOUNTY HUNTER ecosystem. This is not merely a efootball tournament; it is a live financial market where your skill dictates your net worth & your hunting skills. Your goal is to accumulate Bounty Points (BP) to rise from a Recruit to a Legend.

Phase 1 FOUNDATION

The accumulation phase. You will play a set of randomized league fixtures to establish your base value. Every win adds BP to your account; every loss deducts it.

Phase 2 THE HUNTER ARENA

The auto-scheduler turns off. You become the hunter. You must negotiate matches with rivals directly.

• Title Match: Risk 30% of the total pool. (Max 3 matches)
• Standard: Risk 15% of the total pool. (Max 2 matches)

Phase 3 ELITE 8

When the market closes, only the Top 8 Highest Valued Hunters qualify. They enter a ruthless knockout bracket to determine the Grand Champion. All others are eliminated.

Tie-Breaker Protocol

In the event that two or more Hunters hold the exact same Bounty Point (BP) total, the system will automatically determine the higher rank based on the following strict hierarchy:

Priority 1 Total Bounty (BP)
Priority 2
Goal Difference (GD) Formula: (Goals Scored - Goals Conceded)
Priority 3 Total Goals Scored
Priority 4 Total Matches Won

*The leaderboard updates automatically. A higher Goal Difference is the key to breaking ties.

Overtime Sanctions

Time is money. Every fixture has a strict deadline. If a match is not completed and submitted before the deadline expires, it enters Sanction Mode.

The Late Penalty

10% Per Hour Late

The penalty compounds for every hour the result is delayed past the deadline.

Impact on Players

The Winner

Reward Reduced

Your BP winnings are
CUT by the penalty %.

The Loser

Loss Increased

Your BP loss is
AMPLIFIED by the penalty %.

Example: If you are 3 hours late (-30%), the winner only gets 70% of the prize, and the loser loses an extra 30% BP.

Host Responsibility Protocol

Strict adherence to these settings is mandatory. The hosting player is solely responsible for verifying the configuration before kickoff. Post-match disputes regarding incorrect room settings will be summarily dismissed. Check your settings before you start.

01. Match Protocol

  • 1.1 Verification: Both players must verify the result using their unique SLC-ID. If scores do not match, the match is flagged for Admin review.
  • 1.2 Evidence: In case of a dispute, players must provide screenshot proof of the final scoreline.

02. Arena Rules (Phase 2)

  • 2.1 The Refusal Penalty: You have the right to decline a challenge, but doing so will incur a strict 5% Bounty Deduction from your total. This prevents dodging high-level opponents.
  • 2.2 Fixture Limits: To ensure market fluidity, a single hunter is limited to:
    • 3 Title Match Contracts (30% Risk)
    • 2 Standard Contracts (15% Risk)
  • 2.3 The Stakes Formula: Rewards and penalties are calculated based on the Combined Market Value of both hunters.

    Title Match Example (30% Risk)

    You 1000 BP
    +
    Rival 800 BP
    =
    Pool 1800 BP
    Winner (+30%) +540 BP
    Loser (-30%) -540 BP

    Debt Protocol: If a Hunter has Negative BP, they subtract from the pool.
    Ex: 1000 + (-200) = 800 Pool. Hunting bankrupt targets yields lower rewards.

03. Technical Integrity

  • 3.1 Disconnection: If a player disconnects while losing, the result stands. If the score was level, the Admin decides whether a replay or a 0-0 draw is recorded.
  • 3.2 Admin Fiat: The Tournament Admin holds the master key. Admin decisions regarding bug abuse, harassment, or scheduling conflicts are final and non-negotiable.
